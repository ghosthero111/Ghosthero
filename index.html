GENERAL ENTRY REQUREMENTS FOR NATIONAL DIPLOMA PROGRAMME

National Diploma Programmes Accredited by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE), Alhimsad College of Information Technology and Management Studies offers National Diploma (ND) programmes


Admission into the National Diploma (ND) programme is based on the following:

  1. Register for the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) and select Alhimsad College as the preferred institution.
  2. Minimum UTME score of 100.
  3. O-level requirements: A minimum of five (5) credit passes in O-level results (WAEC, NECO, NABTEB, or GCE), including Mathematics and English Language, and three other relevant subjects, obtained in no more than two sittings.
  4. The screening process includes document verification, an aptitude test, and an interview to assess the applicant's qualifications and suitability for the program.
